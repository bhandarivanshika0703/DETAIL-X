Privacy Policy

At Detail X, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or book our professional detailing services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services effectively, we may collect the following types of information when you contact us, book an appointment, or use our website:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address or location details
  • Vehicle information (make, model, service requirements)

We may also collect limited non-personal information such as browser type, device information, and pages visited to help improve website performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly to operate our business and provide a high-quality customer experience, including:

  • Scheduling and managing service appointments
  • Responding to enquiries and customer support requests
  • Providing service updates, confirmations, and reminders
  • Improving our website, services, and overall customer experience

We do not send spam or unnecessary promotional messages. Any communication you receive from us will be directly related to our services.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your personal data. Your information is handled with care and accessed only by authorized personnel.

  • Secure handling of customer information
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular review of data protection practices

While we strive to protect your information, no online system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

4. When We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your information may only be shared when:

  • Required to complete a service you requested
  • Required by law or legal authorities
  • You have given us explicit permission

5. Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve performance and understand how visitors interact with our site.

  • Understanding website traffic and usage
  • Improving website functionality
  • Delivering relevant advertising when applicable

You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies if you prefer. Please note that some website features may not function properly.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your personal information. At any time, you may:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Ask us to update or correct your details
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future communications

7. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for your convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page.
