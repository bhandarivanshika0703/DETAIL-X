Our Story

At Detail X, our journey began with a simple belief — every vehicle deserves more than just a wash. It deserves care, respect, and attention to detail that brings it back to life.

What started as a passion for automotive perfection quickly evolved into a trusted mobile detailing service serving Alberta and British Columbia. With years of hands-on experience, we’ve perfected the craft of restoring, protecting, and enhancing vehicles inside and out — right at our customers’ doorsteps.

We treat every vehicle as if it were our own. Whether it’s a daily driver, a luxury car, a work truck, or a family SUV, our focus remains the same: deliver premium results, honest service, and an experience that leaves a lasting impression.

At Detail X, it’s not just about cleaning vehicles — it’s about earning trust, building relationships, and helping our customers drive with pride and confidence.
